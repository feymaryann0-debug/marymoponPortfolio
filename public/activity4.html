The Best Movies of all time.

My top 5 movies.

The Notebook

I like The Notebook because it tells a timeless love story that shows how true love endures through time, challenges, and memory.

MONARCH Legacy of Monsters

I like this series because it expands the MonsterVerse by combining human drama with epic monster battles and mystery.

Predator Badlands

I like Predator: Badlands because it delivers intense action and suspense while showcasing the Predator’s brutal hunt in a harsh environment.

PRIMATE

I like Primate because it explores the intelligence and raw survival instincts of primates in a tense and thought-provoking way.

The Dinosaurs

I like The Dinosaurs because it brings prehistoric life to the screen with fascinating visuals and a sense of wonder about Earth’s ancient past.
