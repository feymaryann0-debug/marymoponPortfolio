MARY'S CRISPY FRIED CALAMARES RINGS

Ingredients :

  • 500 grams (1lb) squid tube (calamares) cleaned
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup cold soda water
  • Vegetables oil for frying

Equipments :

  • Mixing bowl
  • Knife and chopping board
  • Frying pan or deep fryer
  • Slotted spoon
  • Paper towels

Methods :

  1. Prepare squids :
    2. Slice squids tube into 1cm (1/2 inch) rings. Pat dry with paper towels
  2. Make Batter :
    3. In a bowl,mix flour, cornstarch, paprika, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil :
    4. Heat oil in deep fryer or heavy pan to 180 degree C (350 degree F).
  4. Fry :
    5. Dip calamari rings into batter, allowing excess to drip off. Fry in small batches for 1 1/2 2 minutes until golden and crispy. Do not overcook, calamaresturns rubbery if fried too long.
  5. Drain and Serve :
    6. Place on paper towels to drain. Serve immediately with lemon wedges and your choice of dipping sauces.
